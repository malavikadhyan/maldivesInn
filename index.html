OUR SERVICES





DINING


With traditional Japanese and Italian restaurants, as well as our Farumathi Restaurant which serves the freshest Maldivian seafood, here at Maldives Inn Island Resort & Spa, we have something to suit every taste.


ACCOMMODATION


We offer a wide selection of accommodation at Maldives Island Resort. From spacious bungalows with direct access to the beach to luxury villas with private hot tubs, each one of our properties is designed to help you make the most of your time in Maldives.








SPA & WELLNESS


The Araamu Spa is a peaceful hideaway where you can indulge in a range of relaxing and rejuvenating spa treatments. Home to a sauna, steam bath and Jacuzzi, and surrounded by swaying palms and tropical gardens, the spa is the perfect setting to nourish the mind and body and soothe the soul.


ACTIVITIES & EXPERIENCES


There are endless new experiences to discover at Maldives Inn Island. Embark on a deep sea fishing trip, explore the nearby coral reefs, or simply relax with a cocktail by the pool - the choice is yours.
